Blue Dragon

Blue Dragons float upside down on the surface of tropical waters and target blue bottle jellyfish.
  • Mature Length: 1 inch
  • Habitat: Temperate and tropical waters
  • Diet: Pelagic Creatures
  • Tastes Like: Chicken

Blue dragons have long black appendages, black and grey stripes on a blue body. They ingest the poisonous cells of the jellyfish, which they then use on their own predators.
